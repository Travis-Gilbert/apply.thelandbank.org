Unable to Continue

This doesn't have to be permanent.

Why can't I continue?

The Land Bank cannot sell to you if you have delinquent property taxes or if you have been through tax foreclosure with the Genesee County Treasurer in the last five years.

What can I do?

If you believe this determination was made in error, or if you have resolved your tax obligations, please contact our office directly. We're happy to help.
